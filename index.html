Our Story

Our Hang Heem Homestay-Cum-Restaurant was started in 2018. The word 'Hang Heem' means 'Raj Niwas', and the inspiration behind keeping this name was that we wanted to give people a similar kind of experience as they can get in a Raj Niwas. We believe in the concept of 'ATITHI DEVO BHAVA', which means 'The Guest is God'. Being hospitable to our guests and make them feel like home is our main motto. The motivation behind starting our Homestay was that we always wanted to know new people, talk to them, learn new things from them and in return give them good memories and a pleasant experience at our place.

The Place

A great place to spend quality time with family and friends. The Homestay offer services at an affordable rate, such as: Caretaker, Hospitable Staffs, Clean Personal Room, Living Room, Lawn, Parking, Kitchen, Laundry, Safe, and many more. Delicious food is served. Most of the tourist spots are nearby. Sightseeing facility is available. Picnic and treks are organised on customers' demand. Bonfire with barbeque chicken is the cherry on the cake. People will get an experience that they can cherish for a lifetime.

The place is known for its beauty. Nearby tourist spot includes: Chapel Church (Oldest church in Pedong), Changey Falls, Durpin Monastery, Delo Hill (Guess what? You can enjoy paragliding out there), Loleygaon and many more. One can even trek to Tinchuley Hill (Mt Kanchenjunga can be seen from the hill), and Damsang Fort. Guide will be provided by us. Our place is famous for its heritage sites having cultural, religious, and historical significance. Various species of Himalayan and lowland birds can be seen around our place. All the arrangements of your tour can be done by us on your request. Make us a part of your journey and have an amazing experience.
